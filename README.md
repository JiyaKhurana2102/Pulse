Overview:
Build an AI-powered campus assistant app or chatbot that helps students and members of your fraternity (or the whole campus) with everyday tasks and info.
Key Features:
Event notifications: Push alerts for fraternity meetings, campus events, deadlines.


Room & resource booking: Reserve study rooms, tech equipment, or meeting spaces.


FAQ chatbot: Answer common questions about campus services, tech help, or fraternity info using AI.
Study groups: Connect users interested in similar courses or projects.


Groups: Join a group and all the group events will be listed in your calendar


Integration: Link with campus calendar, email, or social media for seamless updates.
